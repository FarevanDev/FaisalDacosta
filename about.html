About Us

Welcome to SEN Manny Mentor! I'm Faisal, a skilled mentor and SEN manny with extensive experience working with children who have special educational needs.

With a foundation in Drama and Theatre Studies and a career rooted in hands-on support, I bring creativity, patience, and unwavering commitment to each child I mentor. My mission is clear: to empower SEN children to become independent thinkers and learners who can navigate life with emotional resilience and self-assurance.

Experience & Specializations

My experience includes years spent guiding children with autism, ADHD, and other needs, helping them overcome challenges, build confidence, and develop a genuine love for learning. I specialize in areas such as anxiety management, life skills development, personal growth, and emotional regulation.

Approach & Philosophy

While my expertise is grounded in practical experience rather than formal qualifications, I continuously expand my knowledge through courses like “Understanding Autism” and actively collaborate with NHS Speech and Language Therapists, Occupational Therapists, Educational Psychologists, and SEN tutors.

Commitment to Growth

This commitment to growth ensures my approach is both compassionate and highly effective. Families consistently share positive testimonials about the impact of my work, from easing school anxiety to boosting confidence and fostering independence.

Mission Statement

At SEN Manny Mentor, I create a nurturing, supportive environment where every child can flourish, realize their potential, and step confidently into their future as capable, independent learners.
